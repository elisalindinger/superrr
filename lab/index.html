Superrr Lab is a feminist think tank and consultancy. We help public and private entities in developing ethical technology.

Currently, we are running the first round of the Feminist Tech Fellowship. Together with the Mozilla Foundation, we are exploring how feminist ideas can improve technology research. We support artist, writers and activists to explore alternative futures.

We also hosts a bi-monthly series of meetups in Berlin for women* who work in art, science or who otherwise want to make the world a better place.

About Us

Elisa Lindinger is the director of Prototype Fund. As a trained archaeologist, Elisa has worked at the intersection of culture and computer science.

Julia Kloiber is the Co-Founder of Prototype Fund and the Superrr network. Previously, Julia worked with the Mozilla Foundation, Open Knowledge Foundation Germany and as Consultant for The Engine Room.
